Our Journey

Built on a Foundation of Trust

Florence Nightingale Nursing was founded with the vision of making professional healthcare accessible to families in Ghaziabad — not just in hospitals, but right at home where patients feel most at ease.

Named after the pioneer of modern nursing, we carry her legacy of compassion, skill, and selfless service. Our team of qualified nurses, pharmacists and medical professionals bring that same dedication to every doorstep we visit.

From routine injections to complex wound care, from physiotherapy to emergency support — we've built a service that families in Raj Nagar Extension and across Ghaziabad rely on completely, every single day.

Our Approach

How We Care for You

A simple, patient-centred process from first contact to recovery.

1. You Reach Out

Call or WhatsApp us any time — day or night. Tell us what care you need and where you are.

2. We Confirm & Dispatch

We confirm the service, assign the right professional, and dispatch them to your location promptly.

3. Care at Your Doorstep

Our nurse or professional arrives with all equipment. They introduce themselves, explain the procedure, and begin care.

4. Follow-Up Support

After the visit, we're still available for questions, monitoring and repeat visits as needed — no extra hassle.
